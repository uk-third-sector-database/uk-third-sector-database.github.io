We provide open training materials for individuals wishing to learn how to use data resources relating to civil society organisations in the UK. All training materials are available on [GitHub](https://github.com/uk-third-sector-database/training-materials).

## Introduction to the Spine Data Resources

A series of presentations and interactive Jupyter Notebooks introducing the UK Third and Civil Society Sector Database. Topics covered include an overview of the data resources, financial history data, board member data and procurement data.

- [Presentations](https://github.com/uk-third-sector-database/training-materials/tree/main/presentations)
- [Jupyter Notebooks](https://github.com/uk-third-sector-database/training-materials/tree/main/code)

## Mapping and Understanding the UK Third and Civil Society Sector

A one-day training course delivered to civil servants at the Department for Culture, Media & Sport in December 2025. The course covered how to access, link and analyse administrative data on UK civil society organisations using R, with sessions on the organisational spine, financial history data and procurement data.

- [Webinar slides](https://github.com/uk-third-sector-database/training-materials/tree/main/dcms-training/slides)
- [R code](https://github.com/uk-third-sector-database/training-materials/tree/main/dcms-training/code)
