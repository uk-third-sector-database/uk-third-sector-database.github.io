We provide open training materials for individuals wishing to learn how to use data resources relating to civil society organisations in the UK. Materials include presentations, interactive Jupyter Notebooks for exploring the data, and further reading. They are aimed at researchers, policy makers and civil society organisations interested in understanding the scale and shape of UK civil society.

The training materials are available on [GitHub](https://github.com/uk-third-sector-database/training-materials).
